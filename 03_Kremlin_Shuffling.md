### **The Personnel Shift: May 2024 – May 2026**
The restructuring of the state security architecture ensures the concentration of executive authority over strategic assets.
* **Nikolai Patrushev:** Removed from the position of Secretary of the Security Council on **May 12, 2024**. Transferred to the role of Aide to the President. This reallocation maintains consultative proximity while extracting the actor from direct operational command over national security coordination infrastructure [2.2.1].
* **Sergei Shoigu:** Appointed as Secretary of the Security Council following ministerial reassignment. Active in this capacity as of **May 14, 2026**, executing regional security coordination and external representation functions [2.3.2].
* **Andrei Belousov:** Appointed as Defense Minister. A civilian economist deployed to optimize the defense-industrial base, enforce fiscal centralization, and suppress administrative extraction. The appointment establishes a non-military, bureaucratized control layer over the Armed Forces.

### **The Nuclear Chain of Command Architecture**
The activation corridor for the *Cheget* (nuclear briefing system) requires triple triple-redundant signal generation:
1. **The Executive Center:** Primary authorization code input.
2. **The Ministry of Defense (Belousov):** Administrative clearance.
3. **The General Staff (Gerasimov):** Technical execution and key transmission.

The placement of a civilian technocrat at the ministerial level and the containment of historical military loyalists within consultative bodies minimizes independent political filters, ensuring zero-friction execution.
