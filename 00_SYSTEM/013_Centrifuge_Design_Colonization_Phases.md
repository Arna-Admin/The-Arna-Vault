By 2200, Mars will **not** be a green planet with a breathable atmosphere.
Forensic atmospheric physics dictates that triggering a runaway greenhouse effect to vaporize polar CO_2 and thicken the atmosphere requires centuries. Even if the atmosphere is successfully thickened to increase surface temperatures and pressure past the Armstrong limit (6.3 \text{ kPa}), the gas matrix will remain highly toxic, hyper-capnic (CO_2-dominated), and devoid of a global magnetic shield.
 * **Conclusion:** The planetary surface remains an unbreathable, high-radiation desert. Humans 175 years from now still live strictly within closed-loop artificial habitats.
